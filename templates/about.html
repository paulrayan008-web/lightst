About

The Street Light Fault Detection System is developed to automate the process of identifying street light issues using Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN). Traditional manual inspection methods require significant time and manpower, which can delay maintenance and increase operational costs.

This project enables users to capture or upload images of street lights through a web-based application. The uploaded image is analyzed using a trained deep learning model to detect faults such as broken lights, flickering lights, and pole damage.

The system provides accurate predictions along with confidence levels, helping authorities take faster action. It also maintains complaint records and generates reports for better monitoring and management.

By integrating artificial intelligence with public infrastructure management, this project supports smart city initiatives and improves safety, efficiency, and automation in street light maintenance.
